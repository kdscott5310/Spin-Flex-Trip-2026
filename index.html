Mallorca

🌿 Zel Mallorca, Palmanova  ·  Arrive 16:15 · Depart 14:20

Sun May 18 Arrival · Golden Hour · Restaurant Illeta Group of 8 ▼
4:15 PM
✈️Land — Palma Airport (PMI)
Touch down at PMI. Clear arrivals, collect bags. Palmanova is about 20 minutes from the airport.
Pre-book 2 taxis or minibus · ~€25–30/vehicle each way
5:15 PM
🏨Check-in — Zel Mallorca, Palmanova
Beachfront hotel right on the sand. Drop bags, freshen up, get settled. Pool, sauna and gym on-site. The breakfast buffet is rated the best hotel breakfast in Spain.
Ask for sea-view rooms together · +34 971 68 01 50
6:30 PM
🍺First Drinks — Es Carregador Beach
Walk straight out of the hotel to the beach. Grab cold drinks at a beach bar and soak up your first Mallorcan golden hour. Sunset in May is around 9 PM.
Free · 5-min walk from hotel · Save your appetite for Illeta!
8:15 PM
🍽️Dinner — Restaurant Illeta, Camp de Mar
One of Mallorca's most magical dining spots — the restaurant sits on a tiny island connected to shore by a wooden footbridge, completely surrounded by the sea. Standouts: paella, gambas al ajillo, Iberico starters and legendary Sangria.
Request an edge table — arriving at 8:15 PM catches the last golden light over the water.
BOOK NOW — no walk-ins · +34 637 077 870 · ~€45–60/person
Mon May 19 Valldemossa · Deia · Gran Folies Dinner Group of 8 ▼
8:00 AM
🍳Breakfast — Zel Mallorca
Included. Fuel up at the legendary hotel buffet. Today you're heading into the UNESCO Tramuntana mountains. Leave hotel by 8:45 AM.
Pick up rental cars evening before so you leave straight after breakfast
9:45 AM
⛪Valldemossa — Carthusian Monastery & Village
The 14th-century monastery where Chopin and George Sand wintered in 1838. See Chopin's Pleyel piano, their rooms, and an ancient monastic pharmacy. The stone village has sweeping mountain views. Allow 1.5–2 hours.
Buy the combined ticket including the tower — views from the top are the highlight. A live Chopin mini-concert often opens the tour.
~€10/person · 45 min drive · Book at cartuxa.es
12:00 PM
🏘️Deia — Mallorca's Most Beautiful Village
Drive the spectacular cliff road from Valldemossa to Deia — one of the best drives in Europe. A tiny hilltop village of golden stone houses draped in bougainvillea, surrounded by olive groves and mountains plunging to the sea. Climb to the church for panoramic views.
Cala Deia — a hidden pebble beach — is a 10-min walk downhill for a quick swim. Allow 1 hour.
Free · 15 min from Valldemossa
1:30 PM
🥘Lunch — Ca'n Costa (Valldemossa Road)
Perfectly placed between Deia and Valldemossa. Best authentic Mallorcan food on the island — Arroz Brut (aromatic spiced rice), slow-cooked meats, huge portions and the best red sangria you'll ever taste.
Ca'n Costa serves lunch Mon–Sat 1–4 PM. Day 2 lunch is the ideal visit.
~€35–45/person · Call +34 971 612 263 to reserve for 8
3:30 PM
🚗Scenic Drive South — MA-10 Coast Road
One of Europe's great drives — the MA-10 winds along the Tramuntana coast past Sa Foradada rock arch, Banyalbufar terraces and the Port d'Andratx marina.
Stop at Sa Foradada viewpoint (15 mins) — rock arch jutting into the sea · ~1 hr to Port d'Andratx
7:00 PM
🌊Dinner — Beach Club Gran Folies, Port d'Andratx
One of Mallorca's most glamorous beach club restaurants, perched on the rocks above the sea. Spectacular cliffside views, Balearic island vibes. The seafood paella is the standout. Sunset from this terrace is extraordinary.
Book a restaurant table (not just daybeds). Ask for a sea-view spot. Consider a taxi back so everyone can drink freely.
BOOK NOW · granfolies.com · +34 971 671 094 · ~€55–75/person
Tue May 20 Soller Boat Charter · Palma Last Night Group of 8 ▼
8:00 AM
🍳Breakfast — Zel Mallorca
Early start — big day. Fuel up at the hotel buffet then drive north to Port de Soller. Allow 1 hour for the mountain drive. Leave by 8:45 AM.
Take the old Coll de Soller mountain road for spectacular views (adds 20 mins)
10:00 AM
🚃Soller Town — Vintage Tram to Port (Optional)
Park in Soller and ride the legendary 1913 wooden tram down through orange and lemon groves to Port de Soller — 20 minutes of pure nostalgia.
~€8/person each way · trendesoller.com · First departure ~10:10 AM from Plaza de España
11:00 AM
⛵Boat Charter — Port de Soller
Set sail along the dramatic Tramuntana coastline — the most spectacular stretch of sea in Mallorca. Hidden turquoise coves, sea caves, sheer cliffs. Swim at Cala Tuent, explore the secret cave at Cala Deia, anchor at Sa Foradada.
BBQ lunch on board: fresh fish, grilled meats, salads. Drinks included: sangria, Mallorcan white wine, cold beers. Water toys: paddleboards, snorkelling gear, kayaks. 4–5 hours.
BOOK NOW · ~€80–120/person · Request BBQ food + drinks package for 8
⛵ Boat Operators — message all three, take first available:
Sail La Vie (Xico)  saillavie.com · +34 611 708 226
Bonnie Lass Charters (Pete) · +34 654 234 897
Sails Up Mallorca (Alvaro) · +34 646 099 885
4:30 PM
🍦Back in Port de Soller — Explore & Relax
Stroll the horseshoe harbour, grab an ice cream or coffee. Pick up a bottle of Mallorcan Hierbas liqueur or the island's famous orange liqueur as a souvenir. Then drive south for dinner.
7:30 PM
🍷Dinner — Fervor Palma
Carrer de Can Verí 4, Palma Old Town  ⭐ 4.5  · A buzzing, design-forward restaurant in the heart of Palma's old town. Open Tuesdays and great for groups — modern Spanish cuisine, excellent charcoal-grilled meats and creative tapas. Lively atmosphere that flows perfectly into a late-night Palma cocktail bar crawl.
~€35–50/person · Book ahead · grupocardon.com/restaurantes/fervor
10:00 PM
🍸Last Night Out — Palma Old Town
After Landing Cocktail Art — 5 stars, free welcome shot, open until 1 AM
Chapeau Palma — Top-500 worldwide, open until 2 AM
Both in the old town, walking distance from each other.
~€12/cocktail · Walk-in friendly
Wed May 21 La Seu Cathedral · Fly Home ✈️ Group of 8 ▼
8:00 AM
🍳Final Breakfast — Zel Mallorca
One last time at the best hotel breakfast buffet in Spain. Savour it. Check-out is 11 AM — check out by 10:00 AM and store luggage at reception while you head to the cathedral.
Ask to store bags until you collect them on the way to the airport
10:15 AM
🕌La Seu Cathedral, Palma
Palma's breathtaking Gothic cathedral right on the seafront. One of the finest in Europe. Gaudí-redesigned interiors with enormous rose windows casting coloured light across the nave. Allow 45–50 minutes. Then walk the waterfront Paseo Marítimo for one last look at the Bay of Palma.
€9 entry · Book tickets in advance at catedraldemallorca.org · Dress modestly
11:45 AM
🚗Drive to Palma Airport (PMI)
Collect luggage from Zel Mallorca on the way, then head to PMI. Return rental cars at the airport. Allow 90 mins minimum before departure.
~20 min from Palma · Leave by 11:45 AM at the latest
2:20 PM
✈️Flight Departs — 14:20 · Adios Mallorca
Wheels up. Three days of sun, sea, mountains, food, wine and memories. Mallorca has a way of pulling people back. Until next time.
Duty-free pick: Mallorcan Hierbas liqueur or island gin — the best island souvenirs
