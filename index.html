Mallorca

🌿 Zel Mallorca, Palmanova  ·  Arrive 16:15 · Depart 14:20

Photo · Unsplash
Sun May 18 Arrival · Golden Hour · Restaurant Illeta Group of 8 ▼
4:15 PM
✈️Land — Palma Airport (PMI)
Touch down at PMI. Clear arrivals, collect bags. Palmanova is about 20 minutes from the airport.
Pre-book 2 taxis or minibus · ~€25–30/vehicle each way
5:15 PM
🏨Check-in — Zel Mallorca, Palmanova
Beachfront hotel right on the sand. Drop bags, freshen up, get settled. Pool, sauna and gym on-site. The breakfast buffet is rated the best hotel breakfast in Spain.
Ask for sea-view rooms together · +34 971 68 01 50
6:30 PM
🍺First Drinks — Es Carregador Beach
Walk straight out of the hotel to the beach. Grab cold drinks at a beach bar and soak up your first Mallorcan golden hour. Sunset in May is around 9 PM.
Free · 5-min walk from hotel · Save your appetite for Illeta!
8:15 PM
🍽️Dinner — Restaurant Illeta, Camp de Mar
One of Mallorca's most magical dining spots — the restaurant sits on a tiny island connected to shore by a wooden footbridge, completely surrounded by the sea. Standouts: paella, gambas al ajillo, Iberico starters and legendary Sangria.
Request an edge table — arriving at 8:15 PM catches the last golden light over the water.
BOOK NOW — no walk-ins · +34 637 077 870 · ~€45–60/person
Mon May 19 Sóller Tram · Deia · Ca's Patro March · Gran Folies Group of 8 ▼
8:00 AM
🍳Breakfast — Zel Mallorca
Included. Fuel up at the legendary hotel buffet. Big mountain day ahead. Leave by 8:45 AM in the rental cars.
Pick up rental cars the evening before so you head straight out after breakfast
9:45 AM
🚗Drive to Sóller Town — Park Here
~50 min drive from Palmanova via the MA-11 mountain road through the Serra de Tramuntana — one of the great drives in Europe on its own. Park in Sóller town centre (Carrer de Sa Mar car park, or roadside on Carrer Gran Via). You'll collect the cars here after the tram ride.
~€3–5/hr parking · Free on side streets · Leave cars here all morning
10:30 AM
🚋Sóller Town → Port de Sóller — Vintage 1913 Tram
Walk from the car park to Plaça de la Constitució (Sóller's main square) and board the legendary open-air wooden tram. Clatters through the narrow village streets, out through orange and lemon groves, and down to the horseshoe harbour at Port de Sóller. 20 minutes of pure nostalgia.
~€8/person each way · trendesoller.com · Departs every ~hour from 08:00
11:00 AM
🌊Port de Sóller — Swim, Coffee & Explore
One of Mallorca's most beautiful harbours — a perfect sheltered horseshoe bay ringed by mountains. Grab fresh orange juice or a coffee at a terrace café, take a dip at Platja de Sóller beach, stroll the promenade. One hour to soak it all in.
Free · Beach right on the waterfront
12:00 PM
🚋Tram Back to Sóller — Collect Cars
Ride the tram back up to Sóller town (20 min) and collect the rental cars. Quick stop for a coffee or pastry on Sóller's beautiful Plaza if needed — it's one of Mallorca's prettiest town squares.
~€8/person · Same tram back — departs every hour
12:30 PM
🏘️Drive to Deia — 15 Min on the MA-10
Short but spectacular drive south on the cliff-hugging MA-10. Deia appears suddenly around a bend — a tiny hilltop village of golden stone houses draped in bougainvillea, olive groves and mountains dropping straight to the sea. Park by the church in the village.
Free · 15 min from Sóller · Park by the church
12:45 PM
🏖️Cala Deia — Beach Time
Walk 10 minutes downhill from the village to Cala Deia — one of Mallorca's most beautiful hidden coves. Crystal-clear turquoise water, dramatic cliffs, pebble beach. Swim, snorkel, soak it in. Ca's Patro March is right here on the rocks — you'll literally walk from the water to your table.
Free · 10 min walk down from village parking · Wear shoes you can walk in
1:30 PM
🦞Lunch — Ca's Patro March, Cala Deia
The Night Manager restaurant — perched on the rocky cliffs of Cala Deia with tables literally at the water's edge. Made world-famous by the BBC series. The freshest seafood in Mallorca — fish caught that morning, grilled simply with lemon and olive oil. Order the grilled calamari, Dover sole, garlic prawns and the Cava Sangria. Allow 2 hours — this is a long, lingering lunch.

⚠️ CASH ONLY · No card machine · ~€50–70/person
Reservations open at midnight — set an alarm · Book at caspatromarch.myrestoo.net · First sitting 1PM (table until 3PM)
4:00 PM
🚗Scenic Drive South — MA-10 Coast Road to Port d'Andratx
One of Europe's great drives — the MA-10 winds along the Tramuntana coast past the Sa Foradada viewpoint (a dramatic rock needle jutting into the sea — 15 min stop), Banyalbufar terraces and the Port d'Andratx marina. Relax, take in the views, and arrive rested for dinner.
~1.5 hrs to Port d'Andratx with stops · Free viewpoints
8:00 PM
🌊Dinner — Gran Folies Beach Club, Port d'Andratx
One of Mallorca's most glamorous beach club restaurants, perched on the rocks above the sea. Spectacular cliffside views, Balearic vibes, and a sunset that'll stop everyone mid-conversation. The seafood paella is the standout — order it for the table. Book a restaurant table (not just daybeds) and ask for a sea-view spot. Consider a taxi back so everyone drinks freely.
BOOK NOW · 8PM · granfolies.com · +34 971 671 094 · ~€55–75/person
Tue May 20 Palma Catamaran · Palma Last Night Group of 8 ▼
8:00 AM
🍳Breakfast — Zel Mallorca
Relaxed morning. Enjoy the hotel buffet then drive to Palma (~25 min). Meeting point: La Lonja Marina, central Palma — arrive 15 min before departure.
11:00 AM
– 3:00 PM
⛵Luxury Catamaran Sailing — Tapas & Welcome Drinks · Max 10–12
Board the Steve Rogers — a 2021 Fountaine Pajot Isla 40, 12m long and 7m wide. Max 10 guests so with 8 of you it's essentially a private charter. Skipper Thorsten and hostess Klaudia run the show — reviewed as some of the best crew in Mallorca.

Sail out of Palma harbour with the Cathedral behind you, anchor at a stunning turquoise bay, swim and snorkel in crystal-clear Mediterranean water, lie on the giant front trampoline, then gather at the shaded cockpit table for tapas.

🥂 Included: Champagne welcome drink on departure · Fresh homemade Spanish tapas served at anchor · Cold water throughout
🍹 Bar on board: Beer · Wine (red, white, rosé) · Gin & tonic · Rum · Mojito · Sangria · Aperol Spritz · Cava · Cocktails
🏄 Equipment (free): SUPs · Snorkels & goggles · Pool noodles · Swim rings

⚠️ Max 10 guests — 8 of you fills it · Likely to sell out · Book immediately

📅 BOOK NOW on Viator →   sailpalma.com →
7:30 PM
🍷Dinner — Fervor Palma
Carrer de Can Verí 4, Palma Old Town  ⭐ 4.5  · A buzzing, design-forward restaurant in the heart of Palma's old town. Open Tuesdays and great for groups — modern Spanish cuisine, excellent charcoal-grilled meats and creative tapas. Lively atmosphere that flows perfectly into a late-night Palma cocktail bar crawl.
~€35–50/person · Book ahead · grupocardon.com/restaurantes/fervor
10:00 PM
🍸Last Night Out — Palma Old Town
After Landing Cocktail Art — 5 stars, free welcome shot, open until 1 AM
Chapeau Palma — Top-500 worldwide, open until 2 AM
Both in the old town, walking distance from each other.
~€12/cocktail · Walk-in friendly
Wed May 21 La Seu Cathedral · Fly to Ibiza ✈️ Group of 8 ▼
8:00 AM
🍳Final Breakfast — Zel Mallorca
One last time at the best hotel breakfast buffet in Spain. Savour it. Check-out is 11 AM — check out by 10:00 AM and store luggage at reception while you head to the cathedral.
Ask to store bags until you collect them on the way to the airport
10:15 AM
🕌La Seu Cathedral, Palma
Palma's breathtaking Gothic cathedral right on the seafront. One of the finest in Europe. Gaudí-redesigned interiors with enormous rose windows casting coloured light across the nave. Allow 45–50 minutes. Then walk the waterfront Paseo Marítimo for one last look at the Bay of Palma.
€9 entry · Book tickets in advance at catedraldemallorca.org · Dress modestly
11:45 AM
🚗Drive to Palma Airport (PMI)
Collect luggage from Zel Mallorca on the way, then head to PMI. Return rental cars at the airport. Allow 90 mins minimum before departure.
~20 min from Palma · Leave by 11:45 AM at the latest
2:20 PM
✈️Flight Departs PMI → Ibiza (IBZ) · 14:20 · Adios Mallorca
Wheels up. Three days of sun, sea, mountains, food, wine and memories. Mallorca has a way of pulling people back. Until next time.
Duty-free pick: Mallorcan Hierbas liqueur or island gin — the best island souvenirs

9:30 AM – 12:00 PM
🛍️Passeig de Gràcia — Morning Shopping
Barcelona's premier shopping boulevard — 2.5 hours to do it properly. Zara, Mango, Massimo Dutti alongside Chanel, Louis Vuitton, Gucci, Bottega Veneta, and Loewe. Gaudí's Casa Batlló and Casa Milà exteriors are free to admire along the way — stop for a coffee at any terrace café mid-morning.
🚇 L3 metro: Espanya → Passeig de Gràcia — 8 min, 3 stops, €2.40 · Runs every 4–5 min · Or 22-min walk if the morning is nice
12:00 – 12:30 PM

Jump the L3 metro back to Espanya (8 min), drop all bags at the hotel, then head to El Born for lunch. No lugging shopping bags through the Gothic Quarter all afternoon.

🚕 Taxi Gothic Quarter → Hotel: ~€8, 10 min · Then taxi back to Arcano: ~€8 · Or walk 25 min along the waterfront
